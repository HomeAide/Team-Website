Project Introduction

The modern Age of Technology has created an abundance of technology. There are more devices on the market with a wide range of features that most people are unaware of.

There are millions of people in the United States that are currently having difficulties due to disabilities. The advancement of technology can either help alleviate these disabilities, or assist people with them. Technology can help people complete tasks that have become difficult. However, most of them are unaware that there is technology out there that can help them.

Project Goal

The goal for this project is to help bridge the disconnect people assitive technology and those who need them. This can be accomplished through a mobile app that will provide a user with guidance where needed.

This app will not consider difficulties as synonymous with disabilities. In fact, the app will only consider what tasks have become difficult, and give no regard to any medical diagnosis. This mode of consideration will allow the app to consider if the user would really need assitive technology. In the event that the user doesn't need nor want additional devices in their home, the app will be able to provide advice and guidance.
