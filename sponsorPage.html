AT@Home

The modern Age of Technology has created an abundance of technology. There are more devices out there with a wide range of features that most people are unaware of. These days, it is very common to name off devices made by Amazon, Google, Apple, or Samsung. But, devices made by these manufactures might not have the features that some require. And some people may not know what kinds of features these devices even offer.

A major concern that comes with a disconnect with the technologies on the market is that helpful technologies are left unaware by people who may need them. The elderly and those with disabilities have difficulties that can be remedied or assisted with by these technologies. If only they knew that they existed!

This project is all about helping bridge this disconnect and providing information about these technologies to those who can benefit from them. Not only will users be informed about popular brands, they will also be informed about some of the less popular brands. The goal is to help provide users with technologies that are a best fit for them.

A mobile application will be the approach for this particular problem. The app will utilize a database that will contain information about various technologies and the features that they have to offer. This database will be updated frequently as new technologies become available, or technologies update to include more features. Users will input activities that are the most difficult for them. Based upon this information, the app will access this database and determine what kind of assistive technologies will help them with their difficulties.

It is critical that this app be accessible to all people regardless of disability. The app will include features such as a colorblind mode or text-to-speech to provide accessibility. However, based upon the type or severity of a user's difficulties, they may not need additional technology! The app itself will also be able to provide advise and tips that can assist the user with their troubles!
